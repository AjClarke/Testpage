Test Results



The Results from each person's individual test gives us insight into the inner workings of each individual person in the group, these results can be used for multiple different reasons, it makes it easier to see if an individual is introverted or extroverted and allows each group member to tailor their expectation of an individual group member to ensure it's going to suit their personality. This information can also be used to assist with assigning tasks to individuals for example if there is a research task and it is known that an individual member is a visual learner, they potentially they could be the best person to assign this task to.

From our team results, it's interesting to note that most of us got different results for the Myers-Briggs test except for Alex & Allan who both got the 'Defender' personality type. Our learning styles test also reflects a wide range of different learning styles, which is good for our group allowing different tasks to be allocated to different members of our team that may suit their strengths.

All of our test results are displayed on our individual profiles.
